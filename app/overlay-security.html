Manage your Security

Change your UCLA Logon ID Password

Your new password must be different from the old password, between 8 and 128 characters long, is case-sensitive, and must use at least one character from two of the three sets of allowed characters:

  • letters: a-z or A-Z
  • numbers: 0-9
  • special characters: !@#$%^&*().,

Set your Challenge Phrase

A Challenge Phrase allows you reset your password online. Both the challenge and response must be between 8 and 128 characters long. They cannot be the same, and the response is case-sensitive.
