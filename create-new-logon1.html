Select if you have a UID number and are "affiliated" with UCLA or not

You have a UCLA ID if you are a new UCLA Student (including admitted applicants), UCLA Applicant on the waitlist, current UCLA Student, former UCLA Student (including alumni), UCLA Summer Session Only Student (new, current, or former), UCLA Faculty Member or Staff Member, UCOP or UC Merced Staff Member, Contractor, etc.

Accounts created without a UCLA ID number can be used to authenticate to many UCLA applications, but are not generally eligible to receive campus services.
