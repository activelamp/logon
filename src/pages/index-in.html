Welcome to UCLA My Logon

UCLA My Logon is your center for reviewing and managing your digital identity at UCLA. It lays out information and settings relating to your digital identity on campus and digital resources available to you to quickly review. You can either correct or update information in the app, or be directed to where you can request information be changed.

Review your Profile

You can review core information about your digital identity at UCLA like your name, official email, and UID on your profile page.

Manage your Accounts

Your accounts (with the UCLA Logon ID being the most common) at UCLA grant you access to various resources on campus.

You can also manage your BruinOnline Mail Settings here, with further application services to come.
