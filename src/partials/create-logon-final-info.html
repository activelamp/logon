Your Logon ID:

  • must be between 3 and 15 characters in length.
  • must begin with a letter. The remaining characters can consist of any combination of letters and numbers.
  • is case insensitive. All letters are converted to lowercase.

Choosing your password

Your password safeguards access to your account. Select a phrase that is easy for you to remember while difficult for someone else to guess. A valid password:

  • must be between 8 and 128 characters.
  • must contain at least 1 letter and 1 number or the following special character: !@#$%^&*().,
  • Remember: your password is case sensitive. "A" and "a" are not interchangeable in your password.

Selecting Challenge Question & Response

Select a challenge and response phrase so that should you forget your password in the future, you may reset it online. The challenge phrase must be at least 8 characters long and may not exceed 128 characters. The response phrase is case sensitive. It can not be the same as your Challenge phrase. It must be at least 8 characters long and may not exceed 128 characters.
