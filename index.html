About the UCLA Logon ID

The UCLA Logon ID is your campus online account. Use your UCLA Logon ID to access campus wifi, VPN, computer labs, and single sign-on enabled services such as Google Apps (email, calendar, productivity tools), Box (cloud storage), MyUCLA, and hundreds of UCLA and national academic/research resources.
