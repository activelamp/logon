Set your Challenge Phrase

A Challenge Phrase allows you reset your password online. Both the challenge and response must be between 8 and 128 characters long. They cannot be the same, and the response is case-sensitive.

Get Help

If you're having problems managing your account, please bring University-issued photo identification to the Bruin OnLine Help Desk located in Suite 124, Kerckhoff Hall. If you are not able to visit the Help Desk, please fax in a completed copy of the UCLA Logon Service Request Form, with all required supporting documentation.
