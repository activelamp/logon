About MFA in UCLA Logon

Multi-Factor Authentiation (MFA) adds extra protection for your UCLA Logon account. With MFA enabled, you'll be asked to take an extra step to confirm your identity when signing in. This extra step prevents a hacker from accessing your account using a stolen password.

> Learn More about MFA

Setting Up MFA with your Smartphone

If you have a smartphone, we recommend downloading the Duo Mobile app on your phone before starting the setup process. You will need to use the app when setting up MFA.

  • Download Duo Mobile from iTunes
  • Download Duo Mobile from Google Play
